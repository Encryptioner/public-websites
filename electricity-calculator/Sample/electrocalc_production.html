💡 Pro Tips for Accuracy
✓
Check Your Appliance Labels
Look for the wattage printed on the appliance or in its manual. It's usually in the specifications.
✓
Consider Seasonal Changes
AC usage varies with season. Calculate for summer and winter separately for accuracy.
✓
Account for Guest Visits
More people = more lights, fans, and hot water usage. Use date range or days override.
✓
Don't Forget Standby Power
TVs, chargers, and devices draw power even when "off". Include them with lower wattage.
